Heart Disease Machine Learning Project


Our Machine Learning project is a prediction on whether or not a person will be diagnosed with Heart Disease. We have chosen this topic because it is a great dataset to create an analysis of existing data to make a prediction, classification, or regression. We used Scikit-learn in Python to build different models on a sample dataset from Clevleand, OH. The sample dataset can be found on this Kaggle page. To read more on the best fit model for our training data and our final output, please click on the Heart Disease Prediction tab on our navigation bar. We also gathered another dataset from Data.world that contains historical U.S. heart disease and heart stroke mortality data. You can find our Tableau visual charts on U.S. heart disease mortality data by navigating to the Data Visualizations tab. Click here to navigate to our Tableau Public Page and view the full workbook. We wanted to cover geographic and biodemo characteristics in the dataset. Overall, we have used the following for our project: PostgreSQL Database, Python Pandas, Sickit-learn, Tableau, HTML/CSS/Bootstrap, Flask App, and Heroku.
